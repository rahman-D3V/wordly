What is a Word Counter?

A word counter measures the number of words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in any text. Whether you're writing an essay, a blog post, or a report, knowing your word count helps you stay within limits and track progress.

Why Use Draftly?

Draftly gives you instant feedback as you type — no button clicks, no page reloads. It's completely free, works offline, and never stores your text. Just open the page and start writing.

Who Is It For?

  • Students — hit essay word limits without guessing
  • Bloggers — estimate reading time before publishing
  • Writers — track daily writing goals effortlessly
  • Professionals — polish emails, reports, and proposals
